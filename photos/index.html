For a couple of years I’ve been learning how to shoot and edit better photos. I’m collecting my favourites from each year for safekeeping and in the hope that some improvement will emerge.

I shoot with a Canon G11 and edit in Aperture. I trim everything to 16 × 9 because every screen I look at photos on has this aspect and constraints are good.

2011

Photos from Stockholm, Port Dinorwic, Copenhagen, Normandy, Islington, Snowdonia and Durham.

2010

Photos from Didsbury, Uppsala, Snowdonia, Stockholm, Gothenburg and the Isle of Angelsey.
