Wyndham's Theatre interior. Livy took me to see a play in Swedish, Krister Henriksson in Söderberg’s Doktor Glas. It was ace.

Harrison's ‘Sea Watch’ No.1 (H4) with its winding crank — the clock that won George I’s Longitude Prize. Enabling sailors to calculate their precise position on the globe, it’s one of the most important objects in the history of time-keeping.
