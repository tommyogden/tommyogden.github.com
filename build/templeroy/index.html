Design of templeroy.com

A site for comics writer and drawer Ben Dorning.

Ben also did the fine illustrations for our Empty Set record As Neat as a New Pin.

My friend Ben Dorning is ‘a comics writer and drawer’. Ben’s comics are great, so I was glad to have the chance to help him out by designing and developing a web home for them — templeroy.com. Go enjoy them.
